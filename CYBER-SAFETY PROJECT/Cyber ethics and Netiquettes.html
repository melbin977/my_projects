Cyber Ethics And Netiquettes

Cyber Ethics

The Internet offers us convenient ways to shop from our homes and offices for almost everything. We can buy and sell new, as well as second-hand goods.While buying amd selling products, each associate should stick to ethical conduct to ensure that no one is cheated or tricked in nay forms. Good ethics lead to the company providing the most reliable information about products or services.

Netiquettes

The term 'Netiquettes' is a combination of two words- Net(Internet) Etiquettes(Good Manners). It is defined as a special set of rules that governs the behaviour of a user communicating online. The communication can be via E-mails,Forums,Blogs, and social networking sites, etc. You should adhere to the same standards of behaviour online that you follow in real life.
